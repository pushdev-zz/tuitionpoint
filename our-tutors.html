Our Tutors

Who are our teachers?
Our teachers are highly-qualified and dedicated NIE-trained teachers who are passionate, dedicated and committed to helping the students learn positively in a group environment and optimize their potential to the fullest.

Primary School Tutors:

Mdm Siti A.

Mdm Siti is friendly and patient. She takes pride in building a good teacher-student rapport. She aims to facilitate student and help them reach their fullest potential. Classes are catered to the students’ needs; filled with interactive learning through games, power point presentations and videos.

Mdm Yanti

“I believe in providing a friendly and conducive learning environment for students.”
Madam Yanti has more than 12 years of solid teaching experience and specialises in primary level English Language and Science. Since joining Tuition Point 5 years ago, she is well liked by her students. She has good rapport with both her students and their parents. Her teaching methods have helped make complicated concepts understandable to her students. She is approachable, motivating and dedicating in helping students to excel to the best of their potential.

Mdm Dian

Madam Dian is fun and caring. She has 5 years teaching experience and loves travelling.
