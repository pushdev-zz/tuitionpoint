Our Profile

Tuition Point is an established well-known private learning centre which has been providing high quality Core Curriculum courses such as English Language, Mathematics, Science and Creative-writing for over 5 years. Through the years, the demand for quality enrichment programmes has grown and so has Tuition Point. We inspire each child to develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills, and uphold excellence in our professional methods of teaching.

Dedicated Tutors

When it comes to taking that next step in your education, your TuitionPoint tutors has the expertise and resources to help you make your dreams a reality. We have a team of experienced NIE-trained teachers who are committed and passionate about teaching. They each play a role to inspire each child to develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Small group classes

We strongly believe in interaction between the teacher and the students, hence our small group classes (max 10 students) allows us to monitor and guide each student closely.

Teaching Methodology

Our teaching methodology is a topical approach as we are based on the latest MOE syllabus while at the same time it contains elements of flexibility to cater to the different needs of different students. Apart from teaching content, method of delivery and examination preparation skills, our holistic system also looks at how reward system, mock exams, regular feedbacks and study tips help us to achieve our objectives.
